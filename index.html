About Me

Dynamic and Personalized Information Provision in Higher Education

Every year, all senior high school students in the Dominican Republic take a standardized high school exit exam (PN). As such, releasing the PN results through the Ministry of Education's online platform provides an excellent opportunity to identify and reach those students with the potential to pursue a college degree. Having established the data infrastructure, we collaborated with the Department of National Examinations and Consilium Bots, a non-profit organization that develops artificial intelligence (AI) tools to improve people´s important educational decisions, to develop a new digital platform with two aims: to provide students with (i) more accessible and informative test score reports (PN scores platform), and (ii) to provide customized guidance with an AI assistant in student´s higher education decision process by exploiting information about higher education degrees most suited to them and the returns to these degrees (HE degrees platform).
